Compilation

There are times when we do things unconsciously

Things that are impossible for us to carry

Then we will realized what we did

In an instant we could not recall what we’ve done and said


All we can do is to keep moving forward

Face everything and forget being a coward

Move on until we’ve already coped

Continue until we’re at the end of the rope


In this situation then we can admit

Yes we learn a lot and we thought to ourselves

What we’ve face becomes our strength

Either from the past or from our present


We will keep until the end of the road

An experience and a gift given by this world

It is a compilation of our stories

A collection of good and bad memories
