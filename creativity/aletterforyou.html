A Letter For You

A Letter For You

I look up in the sky

I ask my self why?

Why are you so far?

Can't reach you like a star.


All I can do is wait

I am doing it with faith

Everything will gonna be fine

Just how the sun always shine


It brightens up the sky

Always new and never dies

Beautiful like a butterfly

A simple truth and not a lie


We will be together

When all this wait is over

I will hug you once again

Until then I wont complain


Even if I'm missing you

It is impossible for us two

So in this letter I'll say to you

How I miss you and I love you
