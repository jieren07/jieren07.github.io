The Journey to Italy

Detective D headed to the airport.

On his way he noticed that some one is following him.

Suddenly his sight becomes blurry and fell asleep.

Detective D woke up and a little bit groggy.

He's drugged the cause of his sudden blurry sight.

He noticed that he is already outside the country.

How long am I asleep? It is already dark!

He is in a room in Italy! it is in Udine Italy.


D: It seems I got a free travel expense huh!

He look around and noticed that the window is locked and has metal bars installed.

Nobody is in the room! He strided to the door and noticed that it was also locked!

Somebody brought him in Italy and trapped him.

D look around and noticed a cctv installed! Somebody's watching him

D: There's no way out but I have to find a way out!

D walk near a cabinet and open it up.

There is a vault with password! I wonder what's inside.

It is a combination lock!

D input the combination lock after looking at it.
